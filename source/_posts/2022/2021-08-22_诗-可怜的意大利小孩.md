今天我们的午饭是

西葫芦煮通心粉

有一个意大利小孩

端着它，想起了

家乡米兰的晚风

<br>

今天我们的晚饭是

胡萝卜鸡肉盖饭

还是那个意大利小孩

对着它，捏着鼻子

把胡萝卜全挑出去

<br>

2021/8/22

---

A poor Italian kid

<br>

Today our lunch was:

tomato squash pasta.

There's an Italian kid.

The feeling of Milan's wind,

blew through his mind.

<br>

Today our dinner was:

turnip fried rice.

That poor Italian kid,

threw away all the food,

with his nose pinched.

<br>
